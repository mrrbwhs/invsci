HSC Course – Investigating Science

Module 5: Scientific Investigations

Develop, refine, and communicate investigations while considering validity, reliability, and ethical research practices in real-world science contexts.

Module 6: Technologies

Explore how developments in technology influence scientific understanding and how technology is both a product and enabler of scientific discovery.

Module 7: Fact or Fallacy?

Critically examine the reliability of scientific claims and the misuse of science in media, advertising, and public debate.

Module 8: Science and Society

Analyse the impact of science on society, including ethical, economic, and political influences on the direction of scientific research and public perception.
